Abouts us

Founded in 1997 by the current CEO Halvor Bratleif, FiatBank started out as the local IT technical service company Elektro-bistand, based in Jessheim, Norway. After four years of consolidating a local recurring customer base, Elektro-bistand started taking on work for the local municipality, and steadily branched out into business management. In 2002 the company changed name to Fiat Solutions, taking on a broader technological profile.

In 2007, Fiat Solutions bought up the Oslo-based firm Exiver, specializing in ERP and online payment solutions. 2009-10 marked a gradual change in the company service profile towards financial aid services, and in 2011 the company changed name to FiatBank, adopting a profile and strategy geared towards secure modern banking.

In the two decades since, FiatBank has become a respected commercial banking entity, specializing in portable and accessible online solutions and services. With over 200 employees stationed across three countries, FiatBank aims to become the leading trusted consumer-oriented banking service provider of Scandinavia. The core values established in the late nineties haven't changed: Build trust and empower the client through Investing in customer knowledge and autonomy, and provide easy-to-use and reliable financial services or consultation.
